Cooking Method

01

Surface Preparation & Rub

schedule 20m thermostat 45°F
02

Pit Management & Smoke

schedule 6h thermostat 225°F

Establish a clean-burning oak fire. Place brisket fat-side up toward the heat source. Maintain steady temperature between 225°F and 250°F. Spritz with apple cider vinegar every 60 mins after the first 3 hours.

03

The Stall & Wrap

schedule 4h thermostat 165°F

Once bark is set and internal temp hits 165°F, wrap tightly in peach butcher paper. Return to pit until internal temp reaches 203°F and probe tender.
